Help Center

Orders & Shipping

Once your order is placed, you will receive a confirmation email. Orders are typically processed within 24 hours.

Delivery takes 3–7 business days across India. Tracking details will be shared via email once your order is shipped.

During peak seasons or sales periods, delivery may take slightly longer.

Payments

We accept UPI, Debit Card, Credit Card, and Net Banking.

All payments are securely processed through an encrypted payment gateway.

Cash on Delivery (COD) availability may vary by location.

Account & Login

You can create an account by registering with your email or phone number.

If you forget your password, use the "Forgot Password" option on the login page.

You can view your order history inside the My Orders section after logging in.
